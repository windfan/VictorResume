SHORT STORY

This project was from a course that we had to act like a start up company. We had to come up an idea and figure out who our target customers are.

Then we had to interview them based on our prototype app we drew on papers just to know how they will react to our idea or to know is the idea actually solving their problems.

Finally we used last 1.5 months of the semester to start building our Android application.

FEATURES

  • Show the hottest public events nearby.
  • Check the events on categories.
  • Search the events.
  • Pin-point the events.
  • Check the event details, including showing the Google Map on our app.
